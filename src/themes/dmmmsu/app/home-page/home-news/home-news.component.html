The Official Institutional Repository of Don Mariano Marcos Memorial State University (DMMMSU)

Objectives:

  • Promote and provide access to the intellectual properties of DMMMSU and support its aim to become a globally competitive university that transforms human resources with appropriate competencies
  • Preserve the products of scholarly and creative works from the university's instruction, research, and extension programs
  • Disseminate and share the DMMMSU knowledge, research, innovations, and technologies for increased visibility, utilization, and citation
