As part of its commitment to fostering collaboration and enhancing access to resources and research opportunities, the NFRDI Library maintains partnerships and institutional memberships with the following associations:

Institutional Memberships

The International Association of Aquatic and Marine Science Libraries and Information Centers (IAMSLIC) is an organization of librarians and information professionals involved in all aspects of aquatic and marine sciences and their allied disciplines. The association provides a forum to exchange and explore ideas and issues of mutual concern.

Association of Special Libraries of the Philippines (ASLP) is a non-stock, non-profit organization composed of special librarians from government institutions, private sectors, business community and the academe.

The Nutrition Research Information Network (NUTRINET) is composed of institutions whose libraries link up to form a specialized information system in nutrition and related fields.
